the story of a meteoric rise to stardom

From the west-side streets of Toronto to a life of glory, famous throughout the entire northern part of the village of Swansea, Ontario, this is the story of a band who, against all odds, have changed the lives of over 150 people with their smile-inducing, toe-tapping, toddlers-gone-wild style of awesome cover tunes on the best night of the entire year... the annual Ostend Avenue Street Party.
